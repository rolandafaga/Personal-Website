Aloha!

Just an island boy who moved to the
mainland to follow his passions.

Technology has always fascinated me since I was a little kid, and it never fails to amaze me to this day. Growing up in a low-income household, my access to computers and other types of technology was limited however, I always tried to find ways to gain exposure. I’ve come a long way from the kid who created his first website using HTML but I’ve never lost the curiosity and vigor that he constantly maintained, carrying it with me throughout my college journey.

Being born and raised in a small town on the Big Island of Hawaii, where the main industries are agriculture and tourism, I realized that I needed to leave my home in the islands to pursue a better education. Finding a college located in the heart of Silicon Valley aligned perfectly with my goals and visions for the future. Currently, as a second-year student, I am always hunting for opportunities that will help me gain experience in my field.
